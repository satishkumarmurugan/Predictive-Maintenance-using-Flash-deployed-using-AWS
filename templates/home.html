Predictive maintenance involves predicting whether a system or piece of equipment is likely to fail based on input data. In the provided Flask application,the goal is to predict whether a system will fail or not based on various input parameters such as air temperature, process temperature,rotational speed, torque, tool wear, and equipment type. The Flask application utilizes a machine learning model trained on historical data to make these predictions. When users input the relevant data through the application's interface, the model processes this data and provides a prediction on whether the system is likely to fail in the near future. By leveraging predictive maintenance techniques, organizations can take proactive measures to prevent equipment failures, minimize downtime, and optimize maintenance schedules. This can lead to significant cost savings and operational efficiencies by avoiding unexpected breakdowns and maximizing the lifespan of critical equipment.
